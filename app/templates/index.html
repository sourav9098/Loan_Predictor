Loan Approval Process

  1. Application Submission – The borrower submits a loan application with personal, financial, and loan details.
  2. Documentation Review – The lender verifies documents (e.g., ID, income proof, credit history).
  3. Credit Check – The borrower’s credit score and report are evaluated for repayment reliability.
  4. Underwriting – The lender assesses risk, debt-to-income ratio, collateral (if any), and loan terms.
  5. Approval/Rejection – Based on the assessment, the loan is approved, denied, or modified.
  6. Disbursement – Approved funds are released as a lump sum or in installments.

The process may vary depending on the lender, loan type, and borrower profile.
